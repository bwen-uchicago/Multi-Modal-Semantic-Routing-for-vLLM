# Acknowledgments

## Funding

This work is supported by the **National AI Research Resource (NAIRR)** Core AI Track, an initiative led by the [National Science Foundation (NSF)](https://www.nsf.gov/) to democratize access to AI research infrastructure across the United States. NAIRR provides the scalable compute resources essential for training and evaluating the models developed in this project.

For more information about NAIRR, visit [nairrpilot.org](https://nairrpilot.org/).

## Infrastructure

Compute infrastructure for this project is provided through the NAIRR program, with platform support from [Red Hat](https://www.redhat.com/) and the open source community. Red Hat's contributions to NAIRR—including Red Hat OpenShift, OpenShift AI, and enterprise Kubernetes orchestration—provide the project-level isolation and resource management that enable reproducible AI research at scale. See [NAIRR, Red Hat, and open source help provide the control plane for AI research](https://www.redhat.com/en/blog/nairr-red-hat-and-open-source-help-provide-control-plane-ai-research) for details on this infrastructure.
